ABOUT THE BOOK

Namu learns to draw

Six year old Namu learns to draw since her early days. Come join her in this beautiful journey and bring out the artist in you. Find inspiration from everyday things that happen around you.

From the author -

My daughter Namu is really a gifted artist. She started drawing at a very early age. And as a first-time mother, I was so obsessed with her drawings that I used to save all her artwork whether it came from the daycare, or something she would draw after listening to a story, watching a cartoon, after having any experiences or anything she would draw in her school. Since I had a huge collection, I wanted to share it with the world so that they can get some ideas and get their kids started on a similar journey. It's not just about drawing, it is about any passion that any child may have. It could be recording small and beautiful moments of their life, for example - when they first started playing a musical instrument, started writing, learning a language or picking up multiple languages. Not every child is the same, but one thing I am sure everyone will agree with "Every child is gifted", we just have to be open-minded about it, and wait patiently to see what is special for our child.
