Namami Sehgal

Namami Sehgal writes kids books. She lives in Toronto suburbs with her husband and 2 daughters. She just launched her very first book "Namu learns to draw" and is enjoying the present moment.
In theory, she is working on her next fiction book series. In reality, she is out there in the woods jogging, playing with her 2 cute princesses, teaching them some new games or reading books for them.
